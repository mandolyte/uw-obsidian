## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

eaten by worms

#### Glosses:

#### Explanation: 

God's judgment on King Herod Agrippa to be eaten by worms and die was intended to be a particularly demeaning sentence on one who had exalted himself to take the glory that was due only to God
