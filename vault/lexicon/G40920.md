## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to swell up, become swollen (likely the meaning of the medical technical term in Acts 28:6, developed from the meaning "to blow up," that is, "to distend" or "to swell up."  In another related sense, "to burn with fever," "to burn")

#### Glosses:
