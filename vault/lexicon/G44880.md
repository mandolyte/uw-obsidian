## Senses: 

### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

the name of a man in the ancestral line of Jesus Christ

#### Glosses: 

Rhesa

#### Explanation: 

Rhesa was the son of Zerubbabel and father of Joanan.  He is recorded in Luke's Gospel as part of the ancestral line of Christ
