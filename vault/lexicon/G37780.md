### Sense  1.1: 

#### Definition: 

that is, that is to say, too, and that too, him, partly . . . partly.  (special idiomatic uses.  Tοῦτ’ ἐστιν marking an explanation or a clarification, meaning, "that is," as in Mark 7:2). #### Glosses:

#### Explanation:
