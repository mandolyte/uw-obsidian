## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to decide beforehand, prefer, choose (for oneself).  Frequently, in middle voice (when the subject does and experiences the action, doing the action to himself or herself and thus being both subject and object of the action, or when the subject acts to his or her own benefit).  To take by choice.  In active voice (when the subject is doing the action of the sentence), to take out or bring forth.  

#### Glosses: 

to decide beforehand, prefer, choose (for oneself), take by choice, to bring forth
