## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

literally means oily or fatty and is used figuratively to mean rich 

#### Glosses: 

rich, luxurious, costly things

#### Explanation: 

Literally, oily, fatty.  When used figuratively λιπαρός refers to what is rich, bright, luxurious, splendid, or costly. λιπαρός is used as a substantive in Revelation 18:14. When used as a substantive λιπαρός can be translated as luxury. A substantive is a noun or any other word that functions like a noun in a sentence
