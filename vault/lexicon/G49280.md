## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

distress, anxiety

#### Glosses: 

a narrow place

#### Explanation: 

Literally, the word referred to a place of confinement or bondage; in the NT, it is always used metaphorically to describe the feeling of distress or anxiety that such confinement might cause
