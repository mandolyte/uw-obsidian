## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to be distracted, overburdened, anxious.  Figuratively developed from the literal meaning of "to draw off," "to draw away," "to draw around."  In the New Testament, only used in the passive voice (when the action is happening to the subject of the sentence)

#### Glosses:
