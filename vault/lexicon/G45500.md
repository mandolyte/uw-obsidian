## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

of bad quality, worthless for its intended use

#### Glosses: 

rotten, corrupt, bad, worthless

#### Explanation: 

The term is used primarily in the NT of fruit or fish which, even if gathered, are unsuitable for eating and are best thrown away.  As such, it is the opposite of edible items that are  [καλός](../G25700/01.md) or [ἀγαθός](../G00180/01.md)
