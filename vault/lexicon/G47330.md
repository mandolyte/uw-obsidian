## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

steadfastness, firmness

#### Glosses: 

firmness 

#### Explanation: 

Since this word is used elsewhere to describe a solid, permanent structure, Paul's choice of the word here is intended to highlight the intense stability of the Colossians' faith in Christ
