## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to sow

#### Glosses: 

to sow

#### Explanation: 

Jesus uses the every-day practice of sowing or scattering of seed as a picture of the way the Gospel is sown in people's hearts to bring forth a harvest of faith in Him
