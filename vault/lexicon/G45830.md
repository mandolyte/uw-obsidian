## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to be an epileptic

#### Glosses: 

to be moonstruck

#### Explanation: 

It was widely believed in ancient times that epilepsy was caused by the influence of the moon. Hence, an epileptic siezure was caused when the moon "struck" a person
