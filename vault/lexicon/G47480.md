## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to walk in a prescribed path

#### Glosses: 

to be in rows

#### Explanation: 

Originally, the verb described people or things that were lined up in an orderly row, such as soldiers. In time, it took on a metaphorical sense of living one's life ("walking") in an orderly way according to some prescribed set of principles
