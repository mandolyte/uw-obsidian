a terrorist, a politically motivated assassin

#### Glosses: 

a bandit, assassin, one of the Sicarii 

#### Explanation: 

This loan-word from Latin referred specifically to one who carried a concealed dagger ("sica") in order to assssinate those opposed to the political ambitions of his group
