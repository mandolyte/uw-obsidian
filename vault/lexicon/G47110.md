## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a basket

#### Glosses: 

basket

#### Explanation: 

Jesus' miracle of feeding five thousand people culminated in the remnants being gathered in a type of basket known as a [κόφινος](../G28940/01.md), whereas His miracle of feeding four thousand people culminated in the remnants being gathered in baskets known as a σφυρίς.  The σφυρίς was a larger type of basket that was even capable of holding a man, so that it was used in Paul's escape from his adversaries in Damascus
