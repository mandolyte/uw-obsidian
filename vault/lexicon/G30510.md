## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a saying or word which comes from God 

#### Glosses: 

a saying, a word, an oracle

#### Explanation: 

a saying or word which comes from God and can be spoken or preached by men. λόγιον always occurs in the plural in the New Testament and can be translated in English as "words," "oracles," or "sayings"
