## Senses:

### Sense 1.0:

#### Definition: 

A city formerly named Paneas but renamed in honor of Tiberius Caesar which stood at the foot of Mt. Hermon and was called Caesarea Philippi to differentiate it from Caesarea by the sea (see Sense 2.0)  

#### Glosses:
