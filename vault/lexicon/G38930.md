## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to rebel, to be disobedient (used intransitively, that is, having no object of the action, without an object of the action, not having a direct object, having no object to receive the action.  Used transitively, having an object of the action, meaning "to embitter," "make angry," "provoke")

#### Glosses: 

to rebel, to be disobedient.  To embitter, make angry, provoke
