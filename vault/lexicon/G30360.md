## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to kill or try to kill someone by throwing stones at them

#### Glosses: 

to throw stones at, to stone to death, to stone

#### Explanation: 

Stoning in Biblical times was often done by a crowd of people.(compare [λιθάζω](../G30340/01.md): to throw stones, to stone to death)
