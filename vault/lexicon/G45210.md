## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

the seventh day of the week as the Jewish Sabbath

#### Glosses: 

the seventh day

#### Explanation: 

The seventh day of the week was blessed and sanctified by God, because He rested that day from all His work of creation [Gen 2:3](gen 2:3)
