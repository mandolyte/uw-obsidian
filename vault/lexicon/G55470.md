## Senses 

### Sense 1.0: 

#### Definition: 

the Christ, or Messiah;

#### Glosses:

Christ; Messiah;   

#### Explanation:

The adjective χριστός (anointed) was used so frequently to refer to Jesus Christ in the NT that it has become a proper noun.
