## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

completely, entirely, utterly. Idiomatically, εἰς τὸ παντελές, literally, "to the complete," meaning "completely," "entirely," "utterly," as in Luke 13:11 (and most likely Heb. 7:25.  Another proposed translation of εἰς τὸ παντελές in Heb. 7:25 refers to completeness of time, that is, temporally meaning "forever," "for all time")

#### Glosses:
