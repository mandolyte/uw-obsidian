## Senses:  


### Sense  2.0: 

#### Definition: 

to lack something that is necessary or important, to need something that is necessary or important

#### Glosses: 

to be in need, lack, to be wanting

#### Explanation: 

The thing that is lacking could be something physical such as food or clothing or it could be a non-physical thing that is lacking and needed such as godly charachter
