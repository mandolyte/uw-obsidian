## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

frequent.  In the neuter plural, πυκνά, used substantively (functioning as a noun) to mean "frequent(ly)," "often" (much). Comparative, πυκνότερον, meaning "more often," or "very often" (so much the oftener)
