## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

Used to speak of dirt as trash to get rid of and not used to refer to dirt as soil

#### Glosses: 

dirt, uncleaness

#### Explanation: 

The term refers to dirt in the sense of filth or waste matter. This word is not used to refer to dirt as soil
