## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

new and therefore unshrunk

#### Glosses: 

uncarded

#### Explanation: 

Freshly woven cloth that had not first been "fulled" (shrunk and thickened by a heating and moistening process) was likely to shrink and tear when being worn in a garment.  To avoid such tearing, the cloth should be "fulled" before being sewn into a garment
