## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a calmness

#### Glosses: 

a calmness, generally of the sea

#### Explanation: 

The imagery of peace resulting from the calming of a previously tumultuous sea resulted in the use of this word metaphoriclly in  extra-Biblical literature to describe the serenity of one's life or one's society apart from turmoil and danger
