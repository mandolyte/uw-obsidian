## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

Used to show something relates to the city of Rome or the Roman Empire, when applied to a person this term indicates that they are a citizen of the Roman Empire, or live in or are from Rome or the Roman Empire

#### Glosses: 

Roman, a Roman
