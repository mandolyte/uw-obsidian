## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

fellow worker

#### Glosses: 

a yoke fellow

#### Explanation: 

The word pictures someone who is laboring together in a yoke [ζυγός](../G22180/01.md) with a person in gospel ministry, pulling in the same direction and bearing half of the load; it is possible that Paul's use in Philippians refers to a proper name
