## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

literally meaning to "be strong" or "be well" and used in the time of the New Testament as a friendly way of saying good-bye at the end of letters

#### Glosses: 

be strong, be strong,  farewell, good-bye

#### Explanation: 

Wishing someone to "be strong" or "have strength" was a common way of saying "farewell" or "good-bye" at the end of friendly correspondence and at the end of letters
