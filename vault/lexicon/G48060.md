## Senses: 

### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to make alive together with 

#### Glosses: 

to make alive or quicken together with

#### Explanation: 

Paul coins this term to describe the manner in which believers, who were crucified with Christ in His death are made alive with Christ in His resurrection
