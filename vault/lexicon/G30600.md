## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a person who talks abusively to other people or about other people, a person who slanders others with their words

#### Glosses: 

slanderer, verbal abuser, abusive person  

#### Explanation: 

When used as a substantive λοίδορος means "a reviler," "slanderer," or "an abusive person." When a word functions as an adjective it is functioning like a noun in a sentence
