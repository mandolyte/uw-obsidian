## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

the name of one of Jacob's sons who was therefore the ancestor of one of the tribes of Israel

#### Glosses:

Gad

#### Explanation:

With the exception of Joseph, each of Jacob's twelve sons became the patriarch of a tribe of Israel that was from that time forward called by his name.  Joseph was represented among the tribes in the person of his two sons, Ephraim and Manasseh
