## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a thorn, splinter

#### Glosses:

#### Explanation: 

The Apostle Paul refers to a "thorn in the flesh" as some malady or condition that caused him constant physical or eomotional pain but that  God had given him to keep him humble
