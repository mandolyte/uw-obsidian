## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

stubornness, unwillingness to repent when faced with God's truth

#### Glosses: 

hardness of heart

#### Explanation: 

The human heart, which is supposed to be sensitive to God and to being corrected by Him is pictured as hardened to Him
