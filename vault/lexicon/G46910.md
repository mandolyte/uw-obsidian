## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

an idle babbler

#### Glosses: 

an idle babbler 

#### Explanation: 

Originally referring to birds who went about scavenging for seeds (a "seed picker"), the term came to be commonly used for an individual who was not gainfully employed but who survived on scraps of food found in the town markets.  Eventually the term took on a slang, metaphorical use to refer to a useless person who spent his time in the marketplace doing nothing but hearing and repeating scraps of news and gossip
