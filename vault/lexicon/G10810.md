## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

that which has been reproduced from its parent, whether referring to men, plants, or animals

#### Glosses: 

offspring

#### Explanation: 

The Received Text uses γέννημα of a man’s harvested crops in [Luke 12:18](luk 12:18)

#### Citations: 

~of men bearing the characteristics of detestable animals such as [ἐχιδνῶν](../G21910/01.md): [Matt 3:7](mat 3:7), [Matt 12:34](mat 12:34), [Matt 23:33](mat 23:33), [Luke 3:7](luk 3:7), ~ of wine as the "fruit" of the vine: [Matt 26:29](mat 26:29), [Mark 14:25](mrk 14:25), [Luke 22:18](luk 22:18),
