## Senses 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to carry over; to pass across, journey through; 

#### Glosses: 

pass through, go through, go by;

#### Explanation: 

This verb occurs only in its middle/passive (deponent) form in the NT, διαπορεύομαι.
