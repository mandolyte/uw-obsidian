## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

The ceremonial sprinkling of blood for purification

#### Glosses: 

sprinkling

#### Explanation: 

Used specifically of the sprinkling of blood for the purpose of purification. In the New Testament ῥαντισμός is only used of the sprinkling of the blood of Jesus that takes away the sin of those who believe in Him
