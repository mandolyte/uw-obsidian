## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

being marked (by), distinguished (with or by).  In a substantive neuter use, that is, when functioning as a noun, meaning "a figurehead" (an emblem, distinguishing marker).  Developed from the literal meaning of "being marked (by)" or "distinguished (with a sign or mark)"
