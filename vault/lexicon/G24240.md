## Senses:

### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

the name of Jesus Christ the son of God

#### Glosses: 

Jesus

#### Explanation: 

Ἰησοῦς is the Greek word for the Hebrew name Joshua and means "Yahweh saves"
