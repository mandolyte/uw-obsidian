## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a worm or maggot that eats decaying flesh

#### Glosses: 

a worm

#### Explanation: 

Jesus' description of judgment as a state where "the worm does not die", drawn from [Isa 66:24](isa 66:24)(LXX), is a picture of the eternal agony of those who die apart from Him
