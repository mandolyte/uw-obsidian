## Senses:

### Sense 1.0:

#### Definition: 

A name which is the Aramaic equivalent to Peter, Cephas is the Aramaic way of saying the Greek name Peter 

#### Glosses:

Cephas

#### Explanation:

Both Cephas and Peter mean “rock”
