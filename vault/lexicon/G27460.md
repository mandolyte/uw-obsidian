## Senses:

### Sense 1.0:

#### Definition: 

To action of expressing great confidence in someone or something(The New Testament uses this word both for good boasting that is done about God or with reference to God and it is also used for bad and prideful boasting about self--In certain passages when καύχησις is used in a good sense it could be translated as "exult in" or "glory in" or something similar)


#### Glosses:

boast, take pride in, exult in, glory in
