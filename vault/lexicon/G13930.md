## Senses 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

Greek personal name;

#### Glosses: 

Dorcas; 

#### Explanation: 

In Acts 9:36, 39 it refers to a woman whose Aramaic name was Tabitha.  It was common for Aramaic speakers living in Hellenized cultures to have both an Aramaic and a Greek name.
