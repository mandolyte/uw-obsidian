## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to be awake (i.e., physically alive)

#### Glosses: 

metaphorically, to be alive

#### Explanation: 

Because the NT so frequently refers to physical death as "sleep," the opposite (being physically alive) can be viewed as being awake
