## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

This word refers to things that are made out of wood

#### Glosses: 

wooden 

#### Explanation: 

The thing made of wood could be an eating utensil or an item to hold liquids or could be an idol made out of wood.
