## Senses 

### Sense 1.0: 

#### Definition: 

The commander of a thousand soldiers;   

#### Glosses: 

commander; tribune; commanding officer;

#### Explanation: 

Came to be used in Roman military to refer to a tribune, a commander of a Roman cohort of about 600 soldiers.
