## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a goal or mark on which one fixes his aim

#### Glosses: 

a mark

#### Explanation: 

The term is used in Greek both to refer to hitting a goal in an athletic competition and to a sailor who steers his ship straight into the proper port
