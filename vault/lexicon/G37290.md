## Senses: 



### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to rush, hasten on, rush headlong, run

#### Glosses: 

to rush, hasten on 

#### Explanation: 

used intransitively (without an object of the action), as opposed to when the verb is used causally (when something or someone makes the verb happen or helps it happen) to mean "to set in motion" or "to urge on"
