## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to sprinkle something or someone with something

#### Glosses:

to sprinkle, sprinkle clean

#### Explanation:

In the Mosaic Covenant, sprinkling a person or object with the blood of a slain sacrificial animal ceremonially cleansed that person or object in God's sight

#### Citations: 

~literally, of persons or objects sprinkled with blood under the terms of the Mosaic Law: [Heb 9:13](heb 9:13), [Heb 9:19](heb 9:19), [Heb 9:21](heb 9:21), 

~metaphorically, of the purification from an evil conscience that Christians experience so that they can draw near to God: [Heb 10:22](heb 10:22).
