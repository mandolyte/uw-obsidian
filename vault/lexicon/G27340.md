## Senses:

### Sense 1.0:

#### Definition: 

To reflect something as a mirror does

#### Glosses:

reflect, to reflect, reflect as a mirror, show as in a mirror 

#### Explanation:

In [2Cor 3:18](2co 3:18) Paul could be using κατοπτρίζω with this meaning or with the meaning of Sense 2.0
