### Sense 2.1:

#### Definition:

introduces a question, always in combination with [τί](../G51010/01.md) "why"

#### Glosses:

why …?

#### Explanation:

The construction of διά with the interrogative pronoun τί is literally "on account of what?" and therefore can be translated "why?"


#### Citations:
