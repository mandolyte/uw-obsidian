## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition:

#### Glosses:

#### Explanation: 

Matthew uses the term [βίβλος](../G09760/01.md) [γενέσεως](../G10830/01.md) (the book of the lineage) of Jesus Christ in a clear allusion to the use of the phrase in [Gen 2:4](gen 2:4), [5:1](gen 5:1)
