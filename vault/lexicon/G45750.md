## Senses: 

### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

Augustus, the Roman Emperor

#### Glosses:

Augustus

#### Explanation: 

The term originally referred generally to somone or something that was reverend or august, but eventually was applied almost exclusively to the person of the Roman emperor or things named in his honor
