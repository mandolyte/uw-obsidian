## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to provide for.  In the New Testament, used in the middle voice (when the subject does and experiences the action, doing the action to himself or herself and thus being both subject and object of the action, or when the subject acts to his or her own benefit), developed from the meaning "to foresee"
