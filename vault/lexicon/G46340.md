## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

the feast of Tabernacles

#### Glosses: 

the setting up of tents

#### Explanation: 

The Feast of Tabernacles (or Booths) was a week-long celebration from the 15th to the 21st of the month Tishrei (October 2-9) in which the Israelites were to erect and live in temporary dwellings to remember God's faithfulness in leading them out of Egypt and to enjoy the harvest that had been gathered.  The Feast is described in [Lev 23:34-43](lev 23:34-43) and [Deut 16:13-15](deu 16:13-15).  One of Jesus' important teaching moments about Himself was during the observance of this feast
