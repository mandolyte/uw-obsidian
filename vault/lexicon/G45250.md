## Senses: 

### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to be moved or disturbed from a firm position by someone's fawning attention 

#### Glosses: 

to fawn upon, flatter, beguile

#### Explanation: 

The word is used in common Greek to refer to the wagging of a dog's tail, whereby the dog ingratiates itself to a person and sways that person's actions
