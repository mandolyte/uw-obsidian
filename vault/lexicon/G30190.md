## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a person who is a descendant of Levi and belongs to the Israelite tribe of Levi

#### Glosses: 

a Levite

#### Explanation: 

the tribe of Levi is the tribe God chose to have its males serve in the temple and perform the temple duties
