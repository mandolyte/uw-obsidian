## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

A man in the Old Testament, in Genesis chapter 11, who is in the family line and genealogy of Jesus

#### Glosses: 

Reu

#### Explanation: 

Reu was the son of Peleg and father of Serug.  He is recorded in Luke's Gospel as part of the ancestral line of Christ

#### Citations: 

[Luke 3:35](luk 3:35).
