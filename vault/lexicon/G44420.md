## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition:

fire (used literally to refer to earthly fire.  Used figuratively in certain idiomatic contexts, as of divine wrath in the lake of fire, as in Rev. 20:10, or of causing shame, as in Rom. 12:20)
