## Senses:

### Sense 1.0:

#### Definition: 

The name of a valley and the stream which flows through it for part of the year 

#### Glosses:

Kidron 

#### Explanation:

The seasonal stream flows between Jerusalem and the Mount of Olives and flows into the Dead Sea
