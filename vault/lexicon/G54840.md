## Senses 

### Sense 1.0: 

#### Definition: 

for the sake of; on behalf of; because of;

#### Glosses: 

on account of; for which reason; therefore;

#### Explanation: 

The sg acc form of χάρις is used with a gen noun or pronoun as a preposition; e.g. Eph 3:1, τούτου χάριν, for this reason.
