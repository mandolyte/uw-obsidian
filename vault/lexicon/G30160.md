## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a small copper or bronze coin that was not worth very much

#### Glosses: 

small copper coin, small coin, lepton

#### Explanation: 

in late Greek λεπτός was used as a substantive for a small coin of little worth. A substantive is a noun or pronoun or any other word or group of words that functions like a noun
