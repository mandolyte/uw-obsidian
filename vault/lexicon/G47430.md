## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a tiny point or (when used of time) a brief moment

#### Glosses: 

a prick, a point

#### Explanation: 

When the devil tempted Jesus by condensing the glory of all the world's kingdoms into a brief moment in time, he was obscuring the fact that the glory of every one of those kingdoms had collapsed, leaving his offer fleeting and hollow
