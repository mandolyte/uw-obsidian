## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a little (small) woman

#### Glosses: 

contemptuously, a gullible woman

#### Explanation: 

The term is used in the NT not to refer to a woman's physical stature, but rather to a lack of moral reasoning, which leaves her gullible and vulnerable to being drawn away by men who claim to be serving the Lord but who are really following their own fleshly desires
