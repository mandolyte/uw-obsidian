## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

mark, brand, tattoo

#### Glosses: 

a tattooed mark

#### Explanation: 

Marking slaves as belonging to a particular master was common in Paul's time.  Paul views the bodily scars he received from his persecutors as testifying that he was a bond-slave of Jesus Christ
