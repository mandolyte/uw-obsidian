### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

Pertaining to the region of Galatia

#### Glosses: 

Galatian

#### Explanation: 

In Acts, the mentions of the "region of Galatia" appear to refer to the larger geographical area of "Galatia," rather than to the smaller Roman province by that name
