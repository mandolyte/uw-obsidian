## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

strictly, illiterate, but used to describe those with no formal education

#### Glosses: 

without learning

#### Explanation: 

The description of the apostles as ἀγράμματος is likely to reflect the fact that they had no rabbinic training
