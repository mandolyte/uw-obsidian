## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a slave or captive being set free from their captivity because a ransom price has been given in order to set them free

#### Glosses: 

a redemption, a ransoming, liberation

#### Explanation: 

λύτρωσις is used in a spiritual sense in the New Testament. Used in a general sense λύτρωσις means "a deliverance"
