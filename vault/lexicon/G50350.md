## Senses 

### Sense 1.0:

#### Definition: 

Pertaining to the speed of an activity or event;

#### Glosses:

quickly; at a rapid rate;

#### Explanation:

ταχύ is the neuter, singular form of the adjective ταχύς, -εῖα, -ύ used as an adverb in the NT.
