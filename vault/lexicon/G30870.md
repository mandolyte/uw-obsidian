## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a stand made for putting one or lamps on

#### Glosses: 

a lampstand

#### Explanation: 

λυχνία is often used literally but can also be used figuratively. For example it is used figuratively in Revelation chapter one where the seven lampstands are said to represent the seven churches
