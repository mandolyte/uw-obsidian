## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition:

to live as a citizen, be a citizen, have one's citizenship (literally).  Figuratively used in the New Testament, occurring in the middle voice (when the subject does and experiences the action, doing the action to himself or herself and thus being both subject and object of the action, or when the subject acts to his or her own benefit), meaning "to live," "to conduct one's life"

#### Glosses: 

to live as a citizen, be a citizen, have one's citizenship.  To live, conduct one's life
