## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a little sparrow 

#### Glosses: 

a sparrow

#### Explanation: 

The term is a diminutive of στρουθός (sparrow) and is intended by Jesus to show how God cares even for the smallest, least significant of His creatures
