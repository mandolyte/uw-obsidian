## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

A Moabite woman who married Boaz and was the great grandmother of Kind David and is in the genealogy of Jesus

#### Glosses: 

Ruth

#### Explanation: 

Ruth is one of the main charachters in the Old Testament book of Ruth. Ruth was a Moabite woman who, through marriage, became a part of the people of Israel and of the ancestral line of King David and Jesus
