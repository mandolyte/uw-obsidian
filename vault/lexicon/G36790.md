## Senses:


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to reproach, upbraid

#### Glosses: 

to reproach

#### Explanation: 

to reproach, whether justifiably or unjustifiably.  That is, to disparage or to blame unjustifiably, and hence, to reproach (that is, to insult), or to blame justifiably, and hence, to reproach (to reprimand)
