## Senses: 

### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

To not do what is right

#### Glosses: 

deceit, fraud, wickedness

#### Explanation: 

Used to show recklessnes, fraud, deceit, wrongdoing. Possibly this word could mean to look for an easy or wrong way of doing something and to try to deceive others in order to do the wrong action. This word could mean to not take life and its responsibilities seriously and cosequently to do what is not right
