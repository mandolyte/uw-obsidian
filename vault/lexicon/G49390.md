## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

brought up together

#### Glosses: 

one nourished or brought up with, a foster-brother

#### Explanation: 

Specifically, the term refers to someone who is reared in the same household as another but who is not related to that person by blood
