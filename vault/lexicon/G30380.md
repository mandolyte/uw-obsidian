## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a stone pavement or mosaic

#### Glosses: 

paved with stones, stone pavement, mosaic

#### Explanation: 

The reference in John 19:13 refers to an area in Jerusalem where Pilate judged Jesus. When used as substantive it refers to floor or ground work that is decorated with mosaics. A substantive is a noun or pronoun or any word or group of words functioning as a noun in a sentence
