## Senses:


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

how large, how great.  An interrogative pertaining to large size, "how large," "how great."  Pertaining to a degree of higher status or importance, "how great."  In the New Testament, used in exclamations

#### Glosses: 

how large, how great
