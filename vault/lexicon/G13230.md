## Senses 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

worth two drachma;

#### Glosses: 

 two drachma; coin;

#### Explanation: 

The neuter plural form of this adjective is used in
the NT as a substantive to refer to a coin valued at 
two drachma.  This was about equivalent to a Jewish 
half-shekel,the Jerusalem Temple tax.

#### Citations:
