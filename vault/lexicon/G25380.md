## Senses:

### Sense 1.0:

#### Definition: 

Referring to being recent and new 

#### Glosses:

newness

#### Explanation:

In [Rom 7:6](rom 7:6) καινότητι can be translated as either "newness" or "new way" and in Romans [Rom 6:4](rom 6:4) καινότητι can be translated as "newness"
