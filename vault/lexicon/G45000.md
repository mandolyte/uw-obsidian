## Etymology: 

ῥοῖζος: the noise something passing quickly through the air makes such as the whistling of an arrow. The word ῥοῖζος does not occur in the New Testament

## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

used to describe a rushing sound

#### Glosses: 

with a rushing sound, with a great noise, with a roar, with a loud noise

#### Explanation: 

This word is used by Peter to describe the sound that will be made when the present heavens and earth are consumed by fire
