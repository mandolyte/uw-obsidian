## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a large sword with a wide blade

#### Glosses: 

sword

#### Explanation: 

a large wide sword, used by the Thracians and other non Greek speaking people groups.  In the LXX, the word is used interchangeably with [μάχαιρα](../G31620/01.md)
