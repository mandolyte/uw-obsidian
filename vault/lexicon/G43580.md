## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to anchor, come into harbor, moor, come to anchor near, to bring to anchor at.  In the New Testament, used in the middle (with the subject doing and experiencing the action) or passive voice (with the action is happening to the subject)

#### Glosses: 

to anchor at, come to anchor near

#### Citations:
