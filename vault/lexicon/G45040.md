## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

a male name of Latin origin

#### Glosses: 

Rufus

#### Explanation: 

Rufus was part of the church at Rome and was personally greeted by Paul in his letter to the Romans.  It is possible that this same individual was the Rufus, the son of Simon the Cyrenian, who was compelled to carry Jesus' cross on His way to Golgotha in Mark 15:21
