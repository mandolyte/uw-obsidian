## Senses:

### Sense 1.0:

#### Definition: 

Literally means to burn or sear something with a hot iron, used figuratively in [1Tim 4:2](1ti 4:2) to mean to ruin the conscience so that it no longer is sensitive or works properly

#### Glosses:
