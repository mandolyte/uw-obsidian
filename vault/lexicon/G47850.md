## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to be reckoned to be part of a group

#### Glosses: 

to vote one a place among

#### Explanation: 

The normal sense of this extremelely rare word as involving a vote about an individual is clearly out of place with the context of Acts 1, where the decision was made by the casting of lots. Hence, the sense of merely recognizing the result of that decision is more appropriate
