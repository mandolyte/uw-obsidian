## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

to circumcise (developed from the literal meaning "to cut around," referring to the literal act of cutting the foreskin of a male.  Figuratively, apparently of baptism, as in Col. 2:11)

#### Glosses:
