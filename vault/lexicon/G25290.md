## Senses:

### Sense 1.0:

#### Definition: 

To clearly see something so as to understand it

#### Glosses:

clearly see, perceive, perceive clearly 

#### Explanation:

The meaning of καθοράω involves seeing with the physical senses and also understanding what is seen
