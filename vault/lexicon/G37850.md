## Senses: 


### Sense  1.0: 

#### Definition: 

would that (a form that introduces unattainable wishes)

#### Glosses: 

would that

#### Explanation: 

a fixed form (probably the participle of ὀφείλω, but possibly 2 aor unaugmented), introducing unattainable wishes

#### Citations:
