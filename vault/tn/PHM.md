### τοῦ ἐμοῦ τέκνου & Ὀνήσιμον (1)
“my son Onesimus.” Paul speaks of the way he is friends with Onesimus as if it were the way a father and his son love each other. Onesimus was not Paul’s actual son, but he received spiritual life when Paul taught him about Jesus, and Paul loved him. Alternate translation: “my spiritual son Onesimus” (See: [[rc://*/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
